About Our College

Jamal Mohamed College, founded in 1951, is an autonomous institution affiliated with Bharathidasan University, Tiruchirappalli. Spread over 87 acres, it was established as a minority institution to provide higher education to socially and economically disadvantaged communities.

The college is accredited with an A++ Grade by NAAC (4th Cycle) with a CGPA of 3.69/4 and recognized as a "College with Potential for Excellence" by the UGC.

Our Departments

Established in 1986, the Department offers diverse programs including B.Sc., BCA, M.Sc., and MCA, with AICTE-approved intakes of 120 students annually. It features 7 government-aided and 58 self-financed faculty, advanced labs, over 600 computers, high-speed internet, and dedicated Wi-Fi.
The Department is known for its excellent facilities and high-quality education, earning praise from researchers and academicians.
